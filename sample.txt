Investigation Case: CASE-2024-001

Meridian Holdings Ltd initiated multiple wire transfers totaling approximately $4.2 million during Q3 2024.

The funds were routed through offshore entities registered in the Cayman Islands and British Virgin Islands.

Beneficiary accounts included Apex Ventures LLC and several shell corporations with limited ownership transparency.

Compliance analysts flagged the transactions as potentially suspicious due to unusual transaction velocity, layered transfers, and missing beneficial ownership documentation.

Review recommended for enhanced due diligence and SAR preparation.
