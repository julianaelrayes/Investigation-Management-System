Case ID: CASE-2024-001

Meridian Holdings Ltd processed outbound wire transfers totaling approximately $4.2 million during Q3 2024.

Funds were routed to offshore shell companies in the Cayman Islands and British Virgin Islands.

Beneficiary accounts included Apex Ventures LLC.

Risk indicators include high transaction velocity, layered transfers, incomplete beneficial ownership records, and offshore jurisdictions.

Recommendation: Escalate to AML investigators and prepare SAR.
